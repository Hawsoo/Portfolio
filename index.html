The Portfolio of Hawsoo

Hi.
This portfolio is to show off the projects that we have made and are currently making. Hopefully, this portfolio will end up helping you out as well, especially if you aspire to be a programmer.

Having started to make programs since 2009, we have created many projects. These projects, however, usually don't get finished. That is the dilemma and habit we are facing. Though being self-taught programmers, we have next to nothing to show, which quite frankly, is nothing to show for.

After years of working with different languages, APIs, and IDEs, we have come to have a broad sense of the tools to make something.

But we're not satisfied.

We can make something, and it can be great, but nobody will end up knowing about it; we are in essence "bedroom programmers."

Let's change that.
